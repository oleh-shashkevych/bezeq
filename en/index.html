Social and economic resilience, even in times of crisis

"The past year presented the State of Israel with profound national challenges. Bezeq Group - including Bezeq, Pelephone, yes, and Bezeq International TECH stood at the forefront of efforts to preserve national and communication resilience. During the war, our networks withstood exceptional demand, demonstrating their strength, reliability, and stability. Since October 7th, alongside our routine operations and driven by a deep sense of national responsibility, we have invested over NIS 2 billion in infrastructure exceeding the investment made prior to the war.

Bezeq is not only a business group it is an integral part of Israel's national fabric. Israel's geographical location, combined with our infrastructure investments, positions the Group as a bridge between East and West - contributing to Israel's economic resilience and global standing.

In the reporting year, Bezeq Group recorded annual revenues of NIS 9 billion, with significant growth in the core revenues of Bezeq and Pelephone. Concurrently, Bezeq and Pelephone upgraded over 50% of their customer base to advanced infrastructure and technologies, with over 800 thousand fiber internet customers and over 1.2 million 5G customers. The strategic and business focus on transitioning to advanced infrastructure was also reflected in the continued development of cloud services at Bezeq International TECH and the strong leadership of yes in the internet-based television market, with 473 thousand subscribers.

The flagship project of nationwide fiber-optic deployment has advanced Israel's economy, in both the business and private sectors, elevating the country into the global top ten for internet speeds. Bezeq is nearing completion of this project, with 2.8 million households already connected.

Alongside our investments in core infrastructure, we never forget those affected since October 7th, and we are deeply committed to supporting individuals, families, and communities. We stand beside our valued employees serving in the reserve forces, work to restore infrastructure in affected areas, and provide ongoing support to bereaved families. These efforts are an integral part of our values and will continue to guide us moving forward.

We hope for the safe return of our hostages and soldiers, and for calmer and better days ahead."

Tomer Raved, Chairman of Bezeq Group

Connecting Israel

Bezeq provides widespread access to high-speed internet through the deployment of fiber optic technology, reaching 2.57 million households, with plans to expand deployment to 2.9 million households by the end of 2025.

yes maintains its position as a market leader in internet-based television, with over 467 thousand IP subscribers.

Pelephone, the first company to operate an independent 5G network in Israel, continues to lead the country's 5G sector with over 1.2 million 5G customers, and the continuation of nationwide deployment.

Bezeq International TECH enhances Israel's global connectivity through the JONAH submarine cable, which carries 35% of the nation's internet traffic and provides infrastructure that enables global companies to operate in Israel and supports Israeli companies in managing international relations.

*All data is accurate as of the end of the 2024 reporting year.

Bezeq Group and the Swords of Iron war

Since October 7th, Bezeq Group companies, which are defined as an essential enterprise in an emergency, maintain business continuity and ongoing communication services for all residents of Israel, with an emphasis on the south and the north of the country and on the security forces, as part of our concept of national responsibility.

In 2024, Bezeq established the Connected Fund, supporting IDF soldiers injured during their service and helping to strengthen their personal resilience and reintegration into civilian life.

800 Bezeq Group employees who served in the reserves received recognition grants amounting to NIS 4 million.

Bezeq connected over 1,000 shelters in northern Israel to free Wi-Fi.

The purple beret family: yes and Pelephone adopted the Givati Brigade at the beginning of the war.

The documentary film #NOVA, which includes over 200 testimonies of survivors of the Nova massacre, became a symbol of international advocacy and was screened before senior diplomats at the United Nations in March 2024.

Bezeq International TECH adopted evacuated families housed at the Prima Hotel in Petah Tikva.

Once a month, Bezeq International TECH held emotional gatherings with the families of victims from October 7th.

And we never forget, not for a moment, the hostages still held in Gaza
